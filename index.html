Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptile that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of communication amon chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are extremely complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts About Camels

  • Camels have 3 sets of eyelids and 2 rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms

Interesting Facts About Teddy Bears

  • The term "Teddy Bear" came from when Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub on a hunting trip
  • The first teddy bear was made by a German toymaker by the name of Margarete Steiff
  • The Oxford dictionary dates the first use of "Teddy Bear" to 1906

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • The black areas of a panda help it blend into shade and dark spots while the white allows it to hide in snow
  • Panda cubs learn to climb at 5 months old, practicing with their mums
  • Pandas have an extended wrist bone which acts as a thumb to let them hold bamboo firmly

Interesting Facts About Flying Cicadas

  • The diet of a cicada consists of fluids found on the sides of living trees
